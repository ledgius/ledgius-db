# PAYG fixture method

PAYG values are generated using ATO Schedule 1 Scale 2 coefficients for employees claiming the tax-free threshold.

Fixture simplifications:

- Resident employee.
- Tax-free threshold claimed.
- No STSL/HELP.
- No Medicare levy variation.
- No extra withholding.
- No tax offsets.
- The fixture is designed to validate deterministic bank/journal/GL reconciliation and should be compared against the production R-0007 PAYG engine before compliance release.
